## Platform Features

### Heading
**Planned Features of the Online Mock Test Platform**

### Intro Text
The online platform being developed by Ram Anjaneya Student Services is designed to deliver a high-quality, data-driven preparation experience for NEET and JEE aspirants.

### Feature List
> **Designer Note:** Each feature can be displayed as a card or icon + text block

1. **NTA-like exam environment**
   - Online mock tests for JEE (Main & Advanced) and NEET are planned to follow the official NTA pattern, interface, timing, and marking scheme, including full and negative marks.

2. **Large NCERT-aligned question bank**
   - The platform is planned to host a question bank exceeding 1.5 lakh questions in Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, and Biology, aligned with the NCERT syllabus and supported by detailed solutions.

3. **Chapter-wise and difficulty-wise tagging**
   - Questions are planned to be classified by chapter and difficulty level to enable targeted practice and customized test creation.

4. **Smart repetition logic**
   - Correctly answered questions are intended not to be repeated in subsequent tests, while wrongly answered questions are planned to reappear in future tests to reinforce weak areas.

5. **Unique test papers for each student**
   - Each candidate is expected to receive a unique question paper through intelligent randomization and question shuffling, reducing predictability and promoting genuine practice.

6. **Subject-wise difficulty control**
   - Candidates are expected to have the option to set different difficulty levels for each subject, helping them match tests to their current preparation level.

7. **Regular content updates**
   - The question bank is intended to be updated on a regular basis to reflect recent exam trends, question types, and important topics.

8. **AI-based performance analysis**
   - After a minimum number of tests, AI-based analytics are planned to provide subject- and chapter-wise performance insights, accuracy metrics, and suggested areas of improvement.

9. **Fast results with solutions**
   - Results are targeted to be generated within approximately 15 minutes of test completion, with solutions shown for all incorrectly answered questions.
